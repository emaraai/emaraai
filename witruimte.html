The Blank Page as a White Space

What began as a search for the perfect definition for (white) space, ended as a showing of the different forms of space. A showing of non-forms in relationship to what we call form.

I write: I inhabit my sheet of paper, I invest it, I travel across it. I incite blanks, spaces (jumps in the meaning: discontinuities, transitions, changes of key).

Species of Spaces by Georges Perec
